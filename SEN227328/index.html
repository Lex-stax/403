Payment of Dues

In addition to paying at least the required minimum percentage of tuition fees, all students must pay their dues to access the clearance card on the portal. This card will be required upon resumption, so payments should be completed in advance. The dues to be paid include:
  • Faculty/Departmental Due
  • SRA Dues
  • NFCS Dues
  • Medical screening Test (For Freshers)

    • To start the payment, click on Pay Dues

    INFORMATION TO THE FRESH STUDENTS

    All fresh(UTME, DIRECT ENTRY, TRANSFER) students are to resume to the University with four(4) photocopies of the under listed documents:
    1. UTME result slip
    2. O' level Result(s)(WAEC/NECO/NABTEB etc)
    3. Veritas University admission letter
    4. Birth certificate/ evidence of age
    5. Attestation letter
    6. Sponsorship letter
    7. Medical fitness report
    8. Evidence of State of origin
    9. Original O' LEVEL certificate (if ready)
    10. Evidence of Change of Institution into Veritas University, for those who didn't chose Veritas University as first choice University
    11. JAMB Admission Letter
    12. Transcript (for transfer students)
    13. Evidence of transfer fee(for transfer students)

    *NOTE:

    1. ALL CANDIDATES THAT WROTE UTME WITH AWAITING RESULTS/DID NOT MAKE VERITAS UNIVERSITY THEIR FIRST CHOICE UNIVERSITY ARE TO VISIT ANY JAMB CBT CENTER FOR THE CHANGE OF INSTITUTION AND UPLOAD THEIR O' LEVEL RESULT(S ) ON THE JAMB PORTAL BEFORE RESUMPTION.
    2. TRANSFER CANDIDATES ARE TO ALSO VISIT ANY CBT CENTER FOR REGULARIZATION OF THEIR ADMISSION, AND THEREAFTER SUBMIT THE INDEMNITY FORM TO THE VICE-CHANCELLOR'S OFFICE FOR SIGNATURE.
